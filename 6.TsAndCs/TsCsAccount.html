3.1.    Bettabets is bound by the provisions of the FICA, which sets out the requirements in relation to identity establishment and verification procedures, the recording and ongoing monitoring of transactions, the maintenance of records and related matters. All these requirements have been put in place in order to ensure that the betting services offered by Bettabets, are not illegally used as vehicles for money laundering and/or the financing of terrorism or other criminal activities.

3.2.    Where FICA or its associated regulations and instruments specify such procedures and/or restrictions, then Bettabets will operate in accordance with such procedures and/or restrictions. Where FICA does not specify such procedures and/or restrictions, then Bettabets shall implement its own procedures and/or restrictions (subject always to any applicable legislation), to promote good corporate governance and socially responsible betting practices. For further information on FICA please log on to www.fic.gov.za.

3.3.    Bettabets is required by FICA first to establish and verify the identity of all Clients before it may lawfully create a betting account for any such Client. In such cases, as part of the account registration process, prospective account holders will be required to disclose the following personal and related particulars:

          (i)           Full names.

          (ii)          SA Identity Number or Passport Number.

          (iii)         Residential Address.

          (iv)         E-mail Address.

          (v)          Mobile and/or landline telephone number(s), and

          (vi)        The source of the funds to be used for the purposes of the betting account.

3.4.    No betting account will be created unless the identity of the Client who applied for same, has been conclusively established and verified by Bettabets.

(i)      Bettabets will not accept or verify any account with an Asylum Seeker’s Permit or Refugee Permit as means of Identity

3.5.    The following procedures will be followed by Bettabets in relation to a Client in respect of whom proof of identification and residential address is required:

(i)      Where a Client presents his or her identification document (ID or passport) in person, Bettabets shall make a photocopy of such document at Bettabets branches and verify that same is a true copy of the original, which shall be filed and retained for record purposes.

(ii)     Where an identification document is transmitted to Bettabets via scans or by electronic mail, such documents will be required to be current (unexpired in the case of passports) and, if required by Bettabets, to be certified as true copies of the original.

(iii)    A driver’s licence may, at the sole discretion of Bettabets, be accepted as proof of identification, provided that:

(a)               The Client is unable to produce an Identity Document for reasons which are acceptable to Bettabets in the circumstances.

(b)               The driver’s licence is valid/current (has not expired), and

(c)               The driver’s licence reflects on the face thereof –

·           A photograph of the holder.

·           The full names or initials and surname of the holder.

·           The date of birth of the holder, and

·           The Identity Number of the holder.

(iv)    Utility bills and similar documents submitted as proof of residential address shall be dated no earlier than three (3) months prior to the date of submission to Bettabets.

3.6.       By completing the Bettabets online betting account registration form, the Client authorises and irrevocably consents that Bettabets shall be entitled to conduct such identification, credit, and other authentication and/or screening or security checks (including, but not limited to, verifying the Client’s source of funds) that Bettabets may choose to perform and/or is required to perform in terms of the applicable laws.

3.7.       The Client consents that as part of the registration process, and at any time during the period for which the Client may be the holder of a betting account with Bettabets that Bettabets may supply the identifying details and any related particulars provided by the Client to third-party service providers, including, without limitation, identity verification and/or screening agencies, cellular network providers, financial institutions, credit bureaux and/or any other national and/or provincial governmental agencies or statutory bodies.

3.8.      Bettabets will respect the privacy of the Client’s personal information and shall take all reasonable steps to protect the personal information of the Client; provided that Bettabets will not be liable for any loss of data by itself or by any other contracted service providers relied upon. For the purposes of this clause ‘personal information’ shall be defined as detailed in the Protection of Personal Information Act No.4 2013 (POPI). The POPI Act may be downloaded from https://www.gov.za/sites/default/files/gcis_document/201409/3706726-11act4of2013protectionofpersonalinforcorrect.pdf

3.9.       In the event that the Client fails to provide Bettabets with the requisite documentation/information Bettabets shall suspend the betting account until such time as the documentation/information is provided to the satisfaction of Bettabets, or the permanent termination of the betting account, if the Client neglects or refuses to provide the relevant information.

3.10.        Bettabets is required to report single cash transactions above R24,999.99 to the Financial Intelligence Centre (“FIC”) in compliance with the provisions of FICA.

3.11.        Bettabets is further obliged to make reports to FIC in the event of –

(i)      suspicious or unusual transactions, and

(ii)     transactions entered by persons suspected of intending to commit or of having committed any offence contemplated in the Protection of Constitutional Democracy against Terrorist and Related Activities Act, 2004.

3.12.     Bettabets reserves the right to suspend any betting account that has not met FICA requirements.

3.13.     Bettabets may at any time request a Client to produce a hard or soft copy of his/her Identity document or smart Identity card, passport, drivers licence and/or proof of residential address.
